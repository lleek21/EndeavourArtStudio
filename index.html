About me


Endeavour Art studio and Gallery is an independant gallery and working studio based in North Yorkshire, where imagination is brought to life through brush and canvas.


As the resident artist, I create original works using acrylic, oils, and watercolours on both canvas and watercolour paper. Every painting is personally produced and sold directly by me.


A particular interest of mine is creating artwork for children - from adventurous pirate scenes to magical fairy forests.


Browse this website to explore my selected collection and see examples of my work!
